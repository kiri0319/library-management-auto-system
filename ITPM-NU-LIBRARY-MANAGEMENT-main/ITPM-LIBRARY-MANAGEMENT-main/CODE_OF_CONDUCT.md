# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

We are committed to creating a respectful and harassment-free community for everyone.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Focus on technical discussion, not personal attacks.
- Accept feedback professionally and assume good intent.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment or discrimination of any kind.
- Abusive language, insults, or trolling.
- Publishing private information without permission.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing community standards. Violations may result in warnings, temporary restrictions, or removal from collaboration spaces.
